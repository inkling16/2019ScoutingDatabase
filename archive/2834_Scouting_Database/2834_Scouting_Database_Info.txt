This folder contains the 2834 Scouting Databases from 2008 through 2018. 
These were originally posted publicly to chiefdelphi.com. 
They are hosted here with permission from Ed Law, formerly of team 2834. Do not re-host these files without permission from Ed or team 2834. Otherwise, you are free to use them for whatever purpose you wish. 
Information specific to each year's database can be found in an info file in that year's respective sub-folder.
